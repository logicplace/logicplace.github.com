Order of Operations

Exampling the order of operations:

3d7e+1 > 1+2*3 && m < +-3 || a > 3 ? wut : idk, 1

Becomes:

3d7e+1 > 1 + 2 * 3 && m < +-3 || a > 3 ?wut:idk , 1
