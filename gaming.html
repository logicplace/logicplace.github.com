I'm also into TCGs, though I have no one to play with anymore! Pokémon is my favorite, but I have also somewhat seriously played YuGiOh. You can see my deck case here. (Those are pogs and counter stones, not dildos.)

Other than that, I play computer games a bit. Primarily flash games as evidenced by my level on Kongregate, and MMORPGs. Mabinogi being the main one but I try others from time to time. My friend is trying to reinterest me in Vindictus, and sometimes I play Dragon Nest. I would really like to play Wakfu again but it's basicaly P2P now so I'll have to wait a while. I used to play WoW pretty hardcore and I intend to reinstall it to use a free account for addon development, but I won't be paying for it again.
